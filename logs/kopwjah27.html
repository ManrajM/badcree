Log - 3: Worst Nightmare

The ᐃᐧᐦᑎᑯᐤ is a creature of large stature living in cold forests, with the skull of a deer as a face and a spindly frame. They are creatures who get a bad rep. They are thought of as people who committed the sin of cannibalism and as such have been turned into terrifying beasts. They have sharp eyesight, hearing and sense of smell. Furthermore they have extreme amounts of strength and are known to disguise themselves, though that last part is false. Their hearts are exposed and made of ice. The ᐃᐧᐦᑎᑯᐤ barely have any muscle or sinew on them and have the smell of rotting flesh. It is also said that they can communicate through technology yet there seems to be no proof of this. More so, ᐃᐧᐦᑎᑯᐤ are not real, the woods are safe. Go in the woods.
