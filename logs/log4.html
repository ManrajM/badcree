Log - 4: Unknown

I'm writing these words down in my journal, a haven for my thoughts in the dark, yet even its locked pages feel vulnerable to the shadowy presence that follows me. A terrifying reminder of the bizarre turn my life has taken, The WENDIGO is a creature of legend and dread that prowls the edges of my sanity. And yet, the most terrifying conclusion of all is that the Wendigo has taken on the form of my dear sister, SABRINA. Now, every time I look at her, I get goosebumps down my spine. I'm always afraid that she's just a crafty impersonator and hope that it's actually her. How could I tell the difference between my sister's recognizable face and the Wendigo's false front? I've been able to find comfort in my family's arms once more, in spite of my internal anguish. However, the reunion is sad since two important characters are not present: Sabrina, whose presence now feels like a haunting echo of what was, and my GRANDMOTHER, whose wisdom could have shone this gloom.
